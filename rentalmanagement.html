Rental Management
and Management of Special Properties

Based on our decades of experience with our own real estate development within the group of companies, we aspire not just to suggest increases in the value of your properties, but also to deliver them.

With our professionalism and our aim to only present you with the results of the best practices, we provide rental services, profitable management, active management of ancillary costs, as well as clear billing adapted to your needs.

As property managers we are your contact person from A to Z for your properties. We are happy to manage your apartment blocks or individual apartments as capital investments.

We have skilfully grouped the “classic” tasks of rental management:

RENTAL, PROPERTY AND ANCILLARY COST ACCOUNTING

  • Full commercial and technical support for your property
  • Rental, property and ancillary cost accounting
  • Monitoring of rents received, management of accounts
  • Verification of invoices and timely execution of all payments due
  • Monthly rent payment to the condominium owner
  • Annual accounting of operating costs for the tenant (accounting for all apportionable costs)
  • Declaration of monthly or annual surplus income for the owner
  • Investment and management of rental deposits
  • New leases
  • Acceptance and delivery of the apartment upon change of tenant
  • Regular inspection and control of your property
  • Planning, execution and monitoring of maintenance measures
